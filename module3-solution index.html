Smoked Hilsa

IIish or Hilsa is the king of fishes for Bengali.This fish is generally found in sweet river water and possess a distinct silver like shining body.When soft hilsa flesh melts in your mouth,it gives you a feeling nothing other than which should be called 'absolute divine'.Hilsa is not all a common fish here in Europe.It cooking process in steamed cooking,so it is called Bhapa Hilsa. Hilsa is very soft fish.It serves with steamed rice and green chilli.

Shorshe Pabda

Pabda Mach or Catfish belongs to the catfish family.Pabda Mach is super tasty and flavourful and is delicate too.Pabda Mach needs just a little shallow fry and it is good to go.It can also be cooked absolutely without fry.Pabda Mach is an integral part of Bengali cuisine,both in India and Bangladesh.Combination of Pabda Mach and shorshe bata (mustard paste) is just heavenly and very fact makes Shorshe Pabda so popular.
